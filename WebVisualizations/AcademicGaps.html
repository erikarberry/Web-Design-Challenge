Academic Growth Gaps



Analysis


This analysis focuses on the academic growth of black students and the academic growth of white students. We wanted to see if there was a relationship between a school district's overall academic growth and the academic growth gap between black students and white students. Essentially, are they growing all students, or just one subgroup? According to this analysis, it appears that the greater the gap in academic progress of white students vs black students, the greater the overall growth score. Apparently the growth scores are curved relative to statewide peers, which may also explain this linear relationship.
