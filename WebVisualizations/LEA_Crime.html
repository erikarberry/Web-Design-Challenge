Reported Acts by LEA



Analysis


This heat map essentially shows the number of each reportable act of crime per LEA. For example, there seemed to be more crimes reported in Charlotte-Mecklenburg Schools and Wake County Schools, which makes sense considering both districts are the two largest in the state in terms of student enrollment numbers. Note also the greatest frequency of types of reportable crimes in all districts: possession of illegal substance, or known in this chart as "Drugs."
