Equity for Students with Disabilities



Analysis


By law, students who are identified to have special needs must receive personalized services throughout their learning journey. No matter what their special education status, it is critical that schools do right by students with special needs, or "exceptional children (EC)" as they are referenced in North Carolina's public schools. We wanted to analyze which reported acts of violence were being committed largely by exceptional children, especially compared to their peers in general education. This range chart shows us that exceptional children are reported more than students in regular education to commit an assault on school personnel.
